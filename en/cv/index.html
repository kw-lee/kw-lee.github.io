About Me

I am currently working as a postdoctoral researcher with Professor Lizhen Lin in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Maryland. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Statistics from Seoul National University under the supervision of Professor Jaeyong Lee. I am working on various areas of Bayesian statistics, particularly high-dimensional Bayesian models, Bayesian nonparametrics, asymptotic theory, and Bayesian computational methods.
